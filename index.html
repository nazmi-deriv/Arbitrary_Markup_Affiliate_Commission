Understanding Arbitrary Markup

What is Arbitrary Markup?

  • ✦ Monthly Commission: Paid specifically to API developers.
  • ✦ Markup Fee: This is an additional fee charged by third-party developers on trades completed through their custom apps.
  • ✦ Collection Agency: Deriv automatically collects this markup and handsomely pays it back to developers monthly.
  • ✦ Independent Stream: Distinct and decoupled from affiliate commissions that are based directly on referred clients' general activity.

Deriv Affiliate Programme Plans

📊

Revenue Share

"Affiliates earn a % of the monthly net profit that Deriv makes from referred clients' trading activities."

  • • Applies to Deriv Trader, Deriv Bot, SmartTrader
  • • Rates increase with performance tiers
🔄

Turnover Plan

"Earn commissions based directly on the total contract volume, regardless of winning or losing."

  • • Options: up to 1.5% of trade stakes
  • • Multipliers: up to 40% of trade fees
  • • Lookbacks: up to 0.8% of trade stake
📍

CPA (Cost-per-Acquisition)

EU CLIENTS ONLY

"Receive a one-time fixed payment of up to $100 USD when a client meets specific parameters."

  • • Require deposit of min $100 USD
  • • Funds must be used for active trading

Master Partner Plan

"Senior affiliates recruit sub-partners and earn passive income based on their overall team progress."

  • • Earn 20% of sub-partners' earnings
  • • Direct trading commissions included
